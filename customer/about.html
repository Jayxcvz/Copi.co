About Copi.co

Crafted with passion. Brewed with love.

Welcome to Copi.Co — your go-to source for premium coffee beans, authentic Japanese matcha, and professional brewing equipment. We offer four distinct coffee bean varieties — Arabica, Robusta, Liberica, and Excelsa — each carefully sourced and roasted to bring out their unique flavor profiles.


Our matcha collection features Ceremonial, Premium, and Culinary grades sourced from Uji and Kyoto, Japan. Whether you prefer a traditional whisked bowl or a creamy matcha latte, we have the perfect grade for every occasion. Pair it with our Full Matcha Starter Set or Premium Matcha Set for the complete experience.


From French Presses and Moka Pots to Espresso Machines and Burr Grinders, our equipment lineup is built for everyone — café owners, home baristas, and beginners alike. At Copi.Co, exceptional coffee starts with exceptional ingredients and the right tools.
